About

Welcome to my personal blog. I intend to write and share my thoughts on technology, software engineering, and anything that comes to mind.

Author

I’m Xuebing (pronounced as shoe-bing). I am a senior software engineer at Zendesk, a customer relationship software company headquartered in San Francisco, where I build fault-tolerant distributed systems at scale. Currently, I work on high-throughput low-latency streaming platform using Scala, Apache Kafka, Kubernetes, and Amazon Web Services (AWS).

Previously, I’ve worked at a finance software company as well as a fast-growing startup, where I built complex loan management software and Airbnb-like marketplace respectively.

I’m based in Melbourne, Australia.
